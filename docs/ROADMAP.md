## Deprecation Timeline

### v1.x Series (2024)

- **No deprecations planned**

### v2.0.0 (Q4 2024)

**Breaking Changes**:
- Legacy template format (YAML v1)
- Old API endpoints (renamed in v1.0.0)
- Non-MCP tool adapters (migrate to MCP)

**Migration Period**: 6 months (Q4 2024 - Q1 2025)

## Release Cadence

<div dir="rtl">

### جدول الإصدارات

</div>

- **Major releases** (x.0.0): Every 6 months
- **Minor releases** (1.x.0): Every 3 months
- **Patch releases** (1.1.x): As needed (bug fixes)

### Support Policy

- **Latest major version**: Full support
- **Previous major version**: Security updates only (12 months)
- **Older versions**: No support (upgrade recommended)
